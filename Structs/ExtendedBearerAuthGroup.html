ExtendedBearerAuthGroup

public struct ExtendedBearerAuthGroup<T> : ExtendedEndpoint where T : CorvusModelTokenAuthenticatable

A special type of ExtendedGroup that protects its content with bearer token authentication for a generic CorvusModelTokenAuthenticatable.
